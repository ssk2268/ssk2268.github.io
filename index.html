Things I care about

Sustainability & Climate

Carbon markets, green finance, climate-tech — figuring out where the money goes and whether it's actually doing anything. This is the thread that runs through most of my work and research, from analyzing climate-tech investments at a PE firm to writing about green and brown firms for my senior seminar.

Policy & Diplomacy

Working at the UN during Pakistan's Security Council Presidency was one of the most formative things I've done — sitting in on high-level negotiations, drafting policy briefs on everything from the SDGs to miners' mental health reform. I'm drawn to the places where economics meets governance, development, and international law.

Consulting & Ventures

From early-stage startups to nonprofits to management consulting across three countries — I like the work of turning research into real decisions and helping organizations actually move.
